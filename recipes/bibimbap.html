Bibimbap Recipe

Description

Bibimbap translates to mixed rice with meat and assorted vegetables. It is a classic Korean dish and can be made in endless variations. It tastes great and is healthy too!

Fun Fact: bibimbap was one of Michael Jackson's favorite foods

Ingredients

Meat and Meat Sauce

  • Minced beef
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Minced garlic

Vegetables and Others

  • Seasoned spinach
  • Seasoned bean sprouts
  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Carrots
  • Cucumbers
  • Fine sea salt
  • Steamed rice
  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil

Bibimbap Sauce

  • Gochujang
  • Sesame oil
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Roasted sesame seeds
  • Apple vinegar
  • Minced garlic

Steps

  1. Mix beef mince with meat sauce and marinate the meat
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Mix bibimbap sauce ingredients into a bowl
  4. Cook spinach, bean sprouts, and shiitake mushrooms
  5. Peel and julienne carrots
  6. Cut cucumbers
  7. Make fried eggs
  8. Put rice into a bowl
  9. Add meat, assorted vegetables, bibimbap sauce, and egg on top
  10. Mix the ingredients into the bowl
