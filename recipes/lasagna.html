Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish. It may seem daunting at first to create it, but with a little work, this recipe will be so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
